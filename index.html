Mission Statement

The mission of Angels Acts of Kindness Inc. 501c(3) is to provide an organization of caring people dedicated to serving community needs by providing individuals who may not be eligible for other assistance a hand up in life. This assistance includes people in need and people involved in enhancing our ecological environment.

History

Incorporated in 1995, Angels Acts of Kindness is an all volunteer organization that is providing Disaster Relief Assistance in the U.S., Food Distribution (over 4.5 million lbs. delivered in New Mexico), School supplies to over 16 Title I schools, Redistributed vehicles & properties, Mobile Food pantries (10,000 – 50,000 lbs.), supplying household and life necessities, assisting disabled Veterans referred by VA HUD with household goods, and redistribution of all items received.

Purpose

Angels is organized exclusively for charitable, educational and scientific purposes, including for such purposes, the making of distributions to organizations that qualify as exempt organizations under section 501c(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, or the corresponding section of any future federal tax code. Angels will distribute assistance to needy and homeless individuals through food, cash, and in kind donations (including professional services) training or food and properties as the situation requires. Angels will refer these persons to other sources of assistance. Angels will assist in the research, restoration and monitoring of the ecological systems globally but will focus on the North American Continent. Such assistance will include the obtaining and disbursement of research, monitoring and restoration grants/donations from government and private industry sources. Angels will assist students to obtain tuition and/or research funds in subject area dealing with the ecosystem as funds are available. Angels will coordinate and organize qualified volunteers in efforts to serve in humanitarian and ecological projects. Angels will solicit and participate in fund raising as required to fulfill the above stated purposes. Angels will receipt cash or in kind donations with applicable tax receipts to benefactors in accordance with state and federal law.

Angels Acts of Kindness has been helping the residents at Bear Canyon Estates Independent Retirement Community help others for several years and has been a bright light for both residents and recipients. Bear Canyon Estates has a unique program called 'Seniors Serving Seniors/Society,' which allows residents to contribute to helping making life better for other seniors and society. Bear Canyon Estates residents have donated food, clothing, furniture, and more to Angels Acts of Kindness and will continue to do so for many years. Angels Acts of Kindness then shares with the residents where there items went and who they have helped. It is a win-win partnership because Angels Acts of Kindness can continue to do the much needed work of helping those in need, while allowing Bear Canyon Estates residents the privilege of serving others in the greater Albuquerque community. I would encourage any individual, private business, and/or corporation to be a part of contributing to Angels Acts of Kindness because it is in giving that we truly receive.
